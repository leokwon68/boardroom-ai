🧭 How Boardroom works

        
      

      

        
1. Type any decision or task below. The chief of staff routes it — real decisions get a board debate, simple tasks get one specialist.

        
2. Three directors argue, a red team attacks, the chair rules with a confidence score.

        
3. Like the verdict? Press ▶ Execute — a plan lands in Approvals; approve it and the deliverable is produced (Chairman plans even run for real — posting, deploying).

        
4. Every verdict is recorded in the Ledger and scored against reality later — your board keeps a public batting average.

        
💡 After a verdict you can keep talking — push back and the board updates its ruling. Turn on Autopilot and it convenes itself.
